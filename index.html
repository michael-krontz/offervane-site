How it works

Live on your site in one afternoon.

  1. 01

    Drop the widget on your site.

    Paste one snippet. It works on whatever you've already got (WordPress, Webflow, Carrd, a plain HTML page) and inherits your brand instead of fighting it.

  2. 02

    A seller enters their info.

    Their address and a few quick questions about the property. No account, no friction. The kind of short form people actually finish.

  3. 03

    They get an offer. You get the lead.

    OfferVane pulls real comps, applies your rule, and shows a number on the spot. The seller sees a cash offer; the lead drops into your dashboard with everything you need to follow up fast.

70% gets you a deal in one market and laughed out of another. OfferVane lets you set your own offer percentage and your repair-cost model by property condition, so every offer reflects how you actually buy. Change a number; every future offer updates.

Easy install

Copy, paste, done.

One snippet in your site's HTML. No developer, no plugin to wrangle, no rebuild. Drop the form wherever you want it and point it at your investor ID.

index.html 
<!-- 1. Where the form should appear --><div id="offervane-form"></div><!-- 2. Load OfferVane and mount it --><script src="https://embed.offervane.com/widget.js"></script><script>  OfferVaneLib.mount('offervane-form', 'YOUR_INVESTOR_IDYOUR_INVESTOR_ID')</script>

Illustrative. You'll get your own widget URL and INVESTOR_ID when you start a trial.
